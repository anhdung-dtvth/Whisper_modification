WhisperSign: Technical Research Report

Topic: Deep-Dive into the 3-Stage Training Methodology, Architectural Integration, and Tensor Flow

Targeting Vietnamese Sign Language (VSL) Recognition

1. Architectural Components & Tensor Flow

WhisperSign fundamentally alters the standard OpenAI Whisper architecture by replacing the Audio Mel-spectrogram processing with a custom Spatial-Temporal Skeletal engine. The model is designed to map high-dimensional continuous skeleton geometry into the discrete latent space expected by Whisper's pre-trained Transformer weights.

1.1 The SignLanguageFrontend

The frontend is responsible for downsampling the temporal resolution while embedding the complex spatial relationships of the hands and poses.

Data Transformation Pipeline:

  1. Input Skeletal Tensor: (B, T, J=42, F=7)
    • B: Batch size.
    • T: Sequence length (at 60 FPS).
    • J=42: MediaPipe joint count (21 left hand + 21 right hand).
    • F=7: Feature channels (x, y, z, v_x, v_y, v_z, confidence).
  2. Temporal Patch Embedding: Groups P=4 consecutive frames into a single contextual patch, linearly projected to the model's latent dimension.
    Xpatch = GELU( Linear(P × J × F) ) × Wproj
    Intermediate Shape: (B, T/4, d_model)
  3. ConvSPE (Positional Encoding): Instead of absolute sine/cosine embeddings, depthwise separable 1D Convolutions (Kernel=31) learn relative temporal positioning dynamically.
    Xspe = Xpatch + Conv1Dpoint( GELU( Conv1Ddepth(Xpatch) ) )

1.2 The Hybrid Two-Pass Decoder

To balance low-latency monotonic alignment with high-context global sequence generation, the network utilizes a dual-head approach outputting to a vocabulary of ~1296 VSL glosses.

  • Pass 1 (CTC Decoder): A lightweight linear projection over the Encoder outputs. Formulates frame-by-frame conditional probabilities. Shape: (B, T', V).
  • Pass 2 (Attention Decoder): A full 6-layer Transformer Decoder with causal upper-triangular masking. Generates context-aware tokens. Shape: (B, T_dec, V).

2. The Progressive 3-Stage Training Strategy

A major technical hurdle in adapting Whisper is catastrophic forgetting. If the untrained frontend passes garbage gradients back to the pre-trained encoder, the model diverges. To counteract this, WhisperSign forces a rigorous isolation strategy.

Training Stage Objective Strategy Active Modules Learning Rate Loss Formulation
Stage 1: Warm-up Force the new Frontend to match the latent space distribution of the frozen Whisper Encoder. Frontend ONLY 1e-3 CTC Only
Stage 2: Joint Harmonize all parameters. Bridge continuous gestures with semantic gloss alignment. ALL Layers 5e-5 Hybrid (CTC + Attention)
Stage 3: Real-time Simulate noisy, continuous streaming environments via heavy temporal jitter and masking. ALL Layers 1e-5 Hybrid (CTC + Attention)

3. Mathematical Formulation of the Optimization Objective

During Stage 2 and Stage 3, the model converges using a weighted Hybrid Loss formulation designed specifically for Sequence-to-Sequence (Seq2Seq) tasks where strict temporal alignment (CTC) and loose semantic translation (Attention) are both required.

The total loss Ltotal is calculated as:

Ltotal = α · LCTC(Y | X) + (1 - α) · LCE(Y | X, Y< t)
  • LCTC: Connectionist Temporal Classification loss. Forces strict, monotonic alignment between the incoming gesture frames and glosses. Handles the "blank" token elegantly without requiring exact frame boundaries.
  • LCE: Cross-Entropy loss from the Attention Decoder. Models the global dependency of the sequence (e.g., standard Vietnamese grammar structure vs. VSL syntax).
  • α: Weight modifier. Experimentally set to 0.3 in the current configuration, heavily favoring contextual accuracy while relying on CTC purely for guiding early alignments.

4. Advanced Data Topologies & Augmentations

Skeletal data from depth sensors is prone to occlusion and timing irregularities. The SignLanguageDataset pipeline corrects this dynamically.

  • Self-Repairing Tensors: 2D CSV/NPY files (T, F_total) are dynamically inferred. The logic identifies F_p=3 or F_p=7 configurations and can automatically extract the hand joints (42) from larger MediaPipe arrays (e.g., cropping 543 Holistic joints down to the strictly required hands).
  • Stochastic Occlusion (Gesture Masking): To prevent overfitting on perfect data, 15% of specific joints and 10% of consecutive temporal windows (up to 10 frames) are zeroed out per epoch.
  • Infrared Noise Injection: Gaussian noise (std=0.005) is added uniformly to replicate the jitter inherent in the Leap Motion Controller's infrared spatial tracking.

5. Conclusion

The WhisperSign architecture represents a highly methodical approach to cross-modal adaptation. By utilizing Spatio-Temporal Convolutions instead of absolute positional encodings, and safeguarding pre-trained knowledge via Stage-Wise freezing, the system achieves stable convergence on complex grammatical structures of Vietnamese Sign Language utilizing raw 3D skeletal data streams.
